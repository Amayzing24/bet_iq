What is moneyline, over/under, and spread? ℹ️
Moneyline: A bet on which team will win.
Over/Under: A bet on the total points scored in the game.
Spread: A bet on the margin of victory or defeat (e.g. Lakers -3 means Lakers win by 3 or more).
